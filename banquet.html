The 2026 ISBA World Meeting

June 28th - July 3rd, 2026
Nagoya, Japan

CONFERENCE BANQUET

The ISBA 2026 Local Organizing Committee warmly invites you to the official Conference Banquet. This special evening is designed to offer participants a memorable experience of Japanese culture, exquisite dining, and excellent networking opportunities with colleagues from around the world.


1. Traditional "Kagami Biraki" Ceremony

A major highlight of the evening will be the Kagami Biraki, a traditional Japanese sake barrel-breaking ceremony. In this auspicious ritual, the lid of a sake barrel is broken open with wooden mallets. "Kagami" refers to the lid of the sake barrel, symbolizing harmony, and "Biraki" means to open, representing an opening to good fortune and prosperity. We are excited to share this vibrant cultural experience with all our international guests!

(Photos of past Kagami Biraki ceremonies will be added here to give you a glimpse of the celebration.)


2. Premium Sake from Fukushima: Kinsuisho

We are proud to feature sake from Kinsuisho (金水晶) for the Kagami Biraki and the evening's toast. Kinsuisho is a highly esteemed brewery from Fukushima Prefecture, known for its rich history and exceptional craftsmanship.

Please note that bottles of this exceptional sake will be available for purchase at the exhibition booths throughout the conference. We highly recommend picking up a bottle as a wonderful souvenir to take home before the conference concludes!


3. Exquisite French Course Dinner

Following the traditional Japanese toast, guests will be treated to an elegant full-course French dinner. Carefully crafted to incorporate high-quality local ingredients, the meal offers a sophisticated culinary experience that perfectly complements the evening's celebrations.


4. Awards Ceremony

The banquet will also serve as the venue for the official ISBA awards ceremony. We will be honoring the outstanding achievements and contributions of our colleagues within the Bayesian community. Please join us in celebrating the award recipients during this special evening.
