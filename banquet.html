The 2026 ISBA World Meeting

June 28th - July 3rd, 2026
Nagoya, Japan

CONFERENCE BANQUET

The ISBA 2026 Local Organizing Committee is pleased to host the official Conference Banquet at the prestigious Nagoya Marriott Associa Hotel. This evening will be a celebration of our global community, featuring Japanese cultural traditions, fine dining, and opportunities to honor the academic achievements of our colleagues.


1. Venue: Nagoya Marriott Associa Hotel

Located directly above Nagoya Station, the Nagoya Marriott Associa Hotel offers stunning views and world-class hospitality. The banquet will be held in one of the hotel's elegant ballrooms, providing a sophisticated backdrop for our final evening together.


2. Special Program & Honors

The banquet will feature a special program dedicated to the ISBA community. This evening provides a formal setting to celebrate the outstanding contributions and remarkable research within the field of Bayesian statistics. Join us as we recognize academic excellence and strengthen the bonds within our global society.


3. Cultural Experience: Traditional "Kagami Biraki"

To celebrate this special occasion, the evening will include a Kagami Biraki, a traditional Japanese sake barrel-opening ceremony symbolizing harmony and good fortune. This vibrant cultural attraction is a long-standing tradition at ISBA meetings held in Japan.

Example of a Kagami Biraki ceremony (ISBA 2012, Kyoto)


4. Exquisite French Course Dinner

In addition to the cultural program, guests will enjoy a refined full-course French dinner prepared by the master chefs of the Nagoya Marriott Associa Hotel. The menu is crafted to showcase high-quality seasonal ingredients, offering a sophisticated culinary experience.
