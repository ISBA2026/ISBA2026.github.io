The 2026 ISBA World Meeting

June 28th - July 3rd, 2026
Nagoya, Japan

CHILDCARE SUPPORT & GRANTS

To ensure that the 2026 ISBA World Meeting is accessible and family-friendly, we are pleased to offer on-site childcare services during the conference. ISBA will subsidize a portion of the childcare costs for all attendees utilizing this service. In addition, further financial assistance (Childcare Grants) is available for early-career researchers and students to help cover the remaining fees.

Service Provider & Venue
The childcare service will be provided by JOYSUPPO, a professional childcare agency in Japan. The service will be located at the conference venue (WINC Aichi) in a dedicated room on the 10th floor, allowing parents to easily check on their children between sessions.

Ages and Operating Hours

  • Eligible Ages: 3 months to 12 years old
  • Operating Hours: The service is available from June 28th to July 3rd, aligning with the conference schedule. (Detailed hours will be provided during the registration process.)

Subsidized Fees for Attendees
ISBA will cover a portion of the cost. Attendees will only be responsible for the following subsidized rates:
  • Half-day (up to 4 hours): 5,000 JPY
  • Full-day (over 4 hours): 8,000 JPY

Childcare Grants
To further support participation, ISBA is offering Childcare Grants to help offset the remaining childcare costs.
  • Eligibility: Priority will be given to early-career researchers (within 5 years of their PhD) and students.
  • Application: Details on how to apply for the grant will be provided soon.

How to Apply for Childcare
If you are interested in using the on-site childcare service, please fill out the preliminary interest form below.
